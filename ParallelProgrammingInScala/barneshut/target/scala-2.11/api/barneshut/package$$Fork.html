Fork

Related Doc: package barneshut

case class Fork(nw: Quad, ne: Quad, sw: Quad, se: Quad) extends Quad with Product with Serializable

Fork represents 4 quadrants: nw, ne, sw and se, each of which is itself a QuadTree. If you divide the cell into 4 sub-cells at the center of the cell, these 4 QuadTrees are the bodies in those 4 sub-cells.

The constructor assumes these 4 sub-cells are the same size and adjacent to each other, and laid out as described cover the space of this cell.

nw

The QuadTree in the top left of the cell.

ne

The QuadTree in the top right of the cell.

sw

The QuadTree in the bottom left of the cell.

se

The QuadTree in the bottom right of the cell.

    Add Body b into the bodies we already have. We have to figure out which sub-cell b will be in, then create a new Fork with b inserted into that sub-cell.

    nw occupies left of centerX and above centerY. ne occupies right of centerX and above centerY. sw occupies left of centerX and below centerY. se occupies right of centerX and below centerY.

    b

    The Body to add to the represented cell.

    returns
